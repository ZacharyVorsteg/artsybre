Photo Guidelines for Best Results

  • High resolution: Original photos from your camera or phone (not screenshots or social media downloads)
  • Good lighting: Natural light without harsh shadows. Avoid direct sunlight or flash.
  • Clear focus: Eyes and key features should be sharp and in focus
  • Multiple angles: Helpful but not required - one great photo is better than many poor ones
