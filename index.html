Meet Bre

Hi there! I'm Bre, and I've been passionate about oil painting for over a decade. There's something magical about transforming a photograph into a hand-painted portrait that captures not just the likeness, but the essence of the subject.

Every brushstroke is applied with care and intention. I work directly with you throughout the process to ensure your painting exceeds expectations. Whether it's a beloved pet, a cherished family member, or a memorial piece, I treat each commission as if it were for my own family.

Based in the USA, I ship worldwide and take pride in delivering paintings that become treasured heirlooms for generations.
