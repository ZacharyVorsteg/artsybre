About

Hi, I'm Bre

I believe every person, every pet, every moment has a story worth preserving. Oil painting allows me to capture not just what someone looks like, but who they are—the warmth in their eyes, the way light falls across their face, the feeling of a cherished memory.

Each commission is a collaboration. I work closely with you to understand what makes your subject special, and I don't consider a piece finished until it feels right.
